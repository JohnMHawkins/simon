OneRedmond Presents

September 13-14, 2019
Redmond, WA

LET'S PLAY!

OneRedmond is hosting a record-setting tournament on the World’s Largest Simon and will be listed in the Guinness Book of World Record as the first World’s Largest Digital Memory Game.

Teams will compete to replicate the sequences of light and sounds by compressing the correct color pads on our 20x20 feet Simon. The top six teams with the highest scores each evening will move on to a final round to determine our nightly winners!

Who can join?

This is an immersive experience suitable for all ages!
